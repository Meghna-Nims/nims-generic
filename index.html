Step-1: Register/Sign Up

  • Register onto Nims Admit Portal with basic details (Name, Email ID, Mobile No., State).email id will act as your username for the portal and password will be set by you after registering on the portal.

  • You will get a mail on your registered email id regarding the Login Credentials.

  • Login to the Nims Admit Portal with the username/password or login through OTP.

Step-2: Complete Your Profile

  • Apply Online with Step-by-Step Instructions.

  • Fill the application form.

Step-3: Make Payment

  • Pay Your Fee Using Application fees of Rs. 1000/- (except Ph.D. and B.Sc. Nursing).

  • For Ph.D. and B.Sc. Nursing pay Application fees of Rs. 2000/-.

Step-4: Upload Documents

  • Upload the documents.

  • Recent Photograph Passport Size Photograph: The dimensions of the photograph should be 3.5 x 4.5cm width.Scanned photograph should be in jpg, gif, png, jpeg, bmp format for online submission

  • Date of Birth proof (Matric certificate)

  • Qualifying exam Certificate/ Marksheet. Attach Class X mark sheet or other qualifying marksheet as applicable. Scanned mark sheet should be in jpg, gif, png, jpeg, bmp, pdf format for online submission.

  • All the submitted documents are to be verified from the originals within the prescribed timelines.

Step-5: Complete Your Registration

  • Deposit the Registration fee within the timelines prescribed by the university

  • Deposit the 25% of First year Tuition fee to get enrolled
